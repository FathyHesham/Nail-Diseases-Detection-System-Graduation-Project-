Nails Diseases Detection Using Image Processing And Deep Learning


Idea

Our project idea is to create an artificial intelligence model that predicts diseases of the body through the nails of the hand The user (patients or doctors) takes a picture of the nail and uploads it to the site, and within a few seconds the model determines the type of disease
